Digital Wellbeing Insight Engine — From screen time data to behavioral understanding

Android's Digital Wellbeing showed users how long they used their phones — but never why. Through user interviews and surveys with urban knowledge workers aged 22–35, I uncovered 4 behavioral patterns driving disengagement. Used RICE prioritization to select the "Why Feed" Interpretation Engine. Defined a north star metric, full GTM strategy, and guardrail metrics to protect against privacy anxiety and addiction risk.
